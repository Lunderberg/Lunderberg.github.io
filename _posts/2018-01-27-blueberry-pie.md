title: Blueberry Pie
tags: [recipes]
---

* 1 baked pie shell
* 1 cup water
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 6 tablespoons butter
* 3 tablespoon flour
* 2 tablespoons vanilla extract
* 1 tablespoon almonds extract
* 1 quart blueberries

Boil water, sugar, butter, and flour.  Cool and add vanilla and
almond.  Pour over blueberries and place in shell.  Not good for
frozen berries.
